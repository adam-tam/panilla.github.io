Welcome to Panilla, the best place for sightseers, mountain climbers, canoers, fishers, and many more! Feel free to browse this page to learn more about Panilla!














Wildlife and Vegetation

Here in Panilla, there are many animals that you can see here! You can experience amazing animals like fish such as salmon and trout, bears, birds such as bald eagles and blue heron, mountain goats, trees, and alpine vegetation, which consists of moss, lichen, and basaltic soils






















Nearby Communities

Panilla is a fairly new town, and people want to know what it's like here, so we decided to find a nearby town to relate to. A town that we found that was close to us in terms of climate and physical space is Bella Coola. Bella Coola is situated in grassland and forestry, and is surrouded by mountain ranges, but the leeward side is facing Bella Coola. In Panilla, there is a river running through it.




















Climate Profile

In Panilla, we pay close attention to the climate and weather so that people know what Panilla feels like. Below are different profiles of climate of Panilla:


  • Average Temperature: 8.91°C
  • Average High Temperature: 12.1°C
  • Average Low Temperature: 4.41°C
  • Average Extreme Maximum: 24.31°C
  • Average Extreme Minimum: -13.5°C
  • Average Yearly Precipitation: 1735mm



















Recreation Potential

There are rivers, which can be used for activities such as Canoeing, Fishing, and other water activities. Also, there are mountains, which open up to sightseeing and climbing. Lastly, there are trees, which can be used for activites like Hiking or activities like Treetop Trekking. Most of the surrounding area are forests, so most of the acttivities are meant for the forest




















Resource Potential

In Panilla, there are three main accessable resources in this region. There are rivers, forests, and mountains, which respectively corresponds to fishing, forestry, and mining. These three resources are quite plenty, so it should sustain Panilla for quite a long time, and also enough to export, too.

























Risk Profile and Factors

There are three natural disasters that are likely to affect the town, and the three disastera are avalanches and landslides. Avalanches and landslides may be common depending on how secure the mountains in terms of the soil integrity and global warming.
